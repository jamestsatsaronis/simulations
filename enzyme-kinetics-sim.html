Enzyme kinetics: Michaelis–Menten

Enzymes (grey) move through solution and capture substrate (blue) at their active site, forming an ES complex (the enzyme darkens) before releasing product (orange). Substrate becomes product only when it is processed at an active site, and every complex that forms goes on to release its product. The progress plot tracks the product actually formed.
